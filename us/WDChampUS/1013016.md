
## Properties associated with the rows of a TreeView control
			



<a name="NOTE1"></a>
<a name="NOTE1_1"></a>


## Overview
<a name="overview_ELTTEXTE000097"></a>
The rows of the TreeView controls found in a window or in a page can be handled by the WLanguage properties.

**Remark**: To handle a treeview row with the WLanguage properties, use:

- The notation: &lt;TreeView name&gt;[&lt;Element index&gt;&gt;]..&lt;Property&gt;

- The notation: &lt;Treeview name&gt; [&lt;Element path&gt;]..&lt;Property&gt;.
