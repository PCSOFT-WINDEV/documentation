
## PrivateKey (Property)

***In french: CléPrivée***
	



<a name="XUse"></a>
<a name="Use"></a>
<a name="description"></a>
The **PrivateKey** property is used to manage private key connections (SSH or MQTT connection). This property is used: 

- by [sshSession](../WDLang3/1000021157.md) variables. 

- by [mqttSession](../WDLang3/1000023023.md) variables.
