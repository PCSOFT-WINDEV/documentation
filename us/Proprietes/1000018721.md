
## MessageID (Property)

***In french: MessageID***
	

<a name="XUse"></a>
<a name="Use"></a>
<a name="description"></a>
The **MessageID** property is used to get and change an email ID. 

This property can be used on:

- the [Email](../WDLang3/3032029.md) structure.

- a variable of type [Email](../WDLang3/1000018713.md).
