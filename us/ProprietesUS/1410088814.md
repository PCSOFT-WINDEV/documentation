
## AttributeSerialize (Property)

***In french: AttributSérialise***
	



<a name="XUse"></a>
<a name="Use"></a>
<a name="description"></a>
The **AttributeSerialize** property gets the serialization name of the type according to its name and the &lt;Serialize&gt; extension attribute. This property is available for: 

- [Definition](../WDLang1/1000019540.md) variables. 

- [Variable Description](../WDLang1/1000019560.md) variables.
