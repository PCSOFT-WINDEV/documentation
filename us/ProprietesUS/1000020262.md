***In french: MessagerieInstantanée***
	



<a name="XUse"></a>
<a name="Use"></a>
<a name="description"></a>
The **InstantMessaging** keyword can correspond to:  

- the **InstantMessaging** property of a variable of type [Contact](../WDLang3/1000020254.md). 

- a variable of type [InstantMessaging](../WDLang3/1000020247.md).
