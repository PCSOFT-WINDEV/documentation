
## Background

***In french: Fond***
	



<a name="XUse"></a>
<a name="Use"></a>
<a name="description"></a>
The "Background" keyword can correspond to: 

- the [Background](../WDLang1/1000022038.md) variable type, 

- the **Background** property of a variable of type [docParagraph](../WDLang1/1000022483.md). 

- the **Background** property of a variable of type [docElementTextArea](../WDLang1/1000023420.md). 

- ![WINDEV](https://doc.pcsoft.fr/ext/images/us/WD.png) the **Background** property of a variable of type [diagSélection](../WDLang1/1410088172.md).
